ABOUT US

CleanScape is a web-based solution empowering individuals to address the waste management problem. Users can capture images of garbage in their locality, upload them to a dedicated webpage with essential details such as name, phone number, and location.The data is seamlessly exported to an Excel sheet, providing municipalities and NGOs with valuable insights for effective waste management.

CleanScape offers a user-friendly interface, simplifying the process of reporting and addressing waste-related issues in local communities. Its intuitive design ensures accessibility for users of all backgrounds and ages, promoting widespread community engagement.
